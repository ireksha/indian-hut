Our Story

A Journey of Spice & Tradition

Nestled across three vibrant locations in Galle, Indian Hut has been welcoming guests since 2010 with the warmth and hospitality that defines true Indian culture.

Our chefs bring decades of culinary expertise — from the tandoor ovens of Punjab to the coastal kitchens of Kerala. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using hand-ground spices and time-honoured family recipes.
